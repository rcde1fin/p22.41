myPAD Web App Help
What do the symbols mean?
stars Exceeded expectations
check_circle Met expectations
contrast Partially met expectations
cancel Did not meet expectations
radio_button_unchecked Still under probationary service
pending No rating
Why are some Rate buttons grey?
A grey Rate button means it is deactivated and cannot be clicked or tapped. This happens when the staff is still under probation. This also happens to a newly regularized staff if they have less than six months service as a regular staff until the year-end appraisal period.
What is the date format of the review date?
The date format is YYYY-mm-dd.

YYYY stands for year e.g. 1900 or 2010.

mm stands for the month e.g. 01 for January or 10 for October.

dd stands for the date e.g. 02 for the 2nd day of the month or 31 for the thirty-first day of the month.
How to rate supervisees
Click or tap on the "Rate" button on the right of the name of your supervisee.
A new window will appear. Choose the rating from the drop down.
Click or tap on the "Submit" button to save the rating to OCS, or click or tap the "Cancel" button to go back.
Do the same for the rest of your supervisees.
